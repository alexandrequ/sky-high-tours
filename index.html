Welcome to Ladakh

Personal journeys through a living Buddhist landscape.

We offer a wide variety of jeep and minibus tours to see the sights of Ladakh, including some of the oldest Buddhist monasteries in the world — many within easy reach of Leh for a day trip, or longer for those who want to stay over.

We also run safaris off the main roads to see wildlife and spectacular scenery, including the high-altitude lakes. For the more adventurous, we support trekking, mountain biking, motorcycle tours, and ascents of Ladakh's mountains — including Stok Kangri, Matho Kangri, Kang Yatse, Mata Peak and Manthok Tsa Peak.

Festival Season

Time your visit to a living dance-drama.

Festivals in Ladakh take the form of cham — sacred mask dances performed by monks in monastery courtyards, representing the purification of mind and the triumph of good over evil, accompanied by drums, conch shells, cymbals and colossal trumpets called dungchen.
