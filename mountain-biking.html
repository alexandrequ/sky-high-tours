Mountain Biking

Ride the world's highest motorable roads — from an easy local loop out of Leh to multi-day missions over the passes to Manali or Srinagar.

Whether you're on a long-range mission to Manali or Srinagar, or want something closer to Leh, we can help organise and support your trip — vehicle backup, route planning and local knowledge of conditions at altitude.

From Leh you can ride over Khardung La, the world's highest motorable road, down into the Nubra Valley — a signature Ladakh ride that pairs sweeping switchbacks with some of the region's most dramatic scenery.

For those wanting more, multi-day routes cross remote passes and valleys rarely seen by road traffic, camping along the way. Every trip is built around your fitness and appetite for altitude — we'll match the route to you.
